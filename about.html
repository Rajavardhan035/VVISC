About IUCEE

IUCEE (Institution of University Council on Emerging Engineers) is a student-driven organization focused on innovation, leadership, research, entrepreneurship, and technical excellence.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements through workshops, seminars, hackathons, industrial visits, and collaborative projects.
